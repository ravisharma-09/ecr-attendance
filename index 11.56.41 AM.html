CR Guide How to Mark & Submit Attendance
1
Select Date & Class
Set the correct date, choose day & batch, then click a class pill to load the student list.
2
Mark Every Student
P = Present  ·  A = Absent  ·  L = Late. Tip: hit All Present first, then mark absences.
L
Late? Set Arrival Time
Clicking L reveals an Arrived at: time field. It auto-fills the current time — adjust if needed.
↓
Export & Send to College
Click Export & Share CSV — downloads a complete sheet with class info, summary & student list. Email/WhatsApp it to faculty the same day.
!
Never Edit the CSV
Do not change roll numbers or names in the file. If correction needed, fix in the app and re-export.
+
Extra / Custom Classes
Not in the timetable? Click + Add Custom Class or go to Manage Classes tab to save it for future use.
Records are auto-saved in this browser when you export. View past records in the Records tab.
